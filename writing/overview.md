## Why This Project Exists

Video games are often dismissed as mere distractions. But they can be so much more. I wanted to explore the nuances of my gaming habits—those late-night binges, the loneliness that sometimes creeps in, and the way gaming can be both an escape and a trap.

This project stems from a need to articulate what gaming has meant to me over the years. It's an examination of what happens when the joystick becomes a crutch, and the line between enjoyment and compulsion blurs. By sharing this narrative, my goal is to resonate with anyone who's ever found solace or struggle in their gaming experiences.

## Trade-offs

The no-frills approach does have its drawbacks. On the one hand, Markdown is incredibly lightweight and easy to manage. On the other, it limits interactivity and graphical presentation. I could have added multimedia elements or interactive content, but I wanted to keep the focus on the words and feelings behind video gaming.

For that reason, it might not appeal to everyone. Some might prefer flashier presentations, but I'm aiming for authenticity over aesthetics here.
